The backstory

the Flying Tomato: An Airborne Accident

T

The Flying Tomato began not with a recipe, but with an epic sibling feud. Brothers Vinny "The Rocket" Rossi and Silvio "The Sauce" Rossi were in a heated, generations-old argument over oregano when Vinny lost it. He grabbed a Perfect Pizza Ball—sacred family dough—and hurled it at Silvio. Silvio ducked, and the dough ricocheted, knocking an open can of San Marzano tomatoes high into the air. The flying can showered Papa Aldo with sauce. Instead of being angry, Papa Aldo was inspired, shouting, "It flies! This is a circus of dough!"

The family immediately started batting the errant can with more dough, turning their kitchen into a battleground. They embraced the anarchy, deciding to open a restaurant where the rules were simple:

  • Dough must fly.
  • The environment must be goofy.

And that's how a projectile of dough and an airborne tomato can created The Flying Tomato, the only pizza place where staff are required to duck.
